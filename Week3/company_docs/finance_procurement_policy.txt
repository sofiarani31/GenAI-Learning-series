Northstar Analytics
Finance and Procurement Policy

Purpose
This policy explains how employees should request purchases, work with vendors, and submit invoices or reimbursement claims.

Purchase Approval Thresholds
Team leads may approve purchases up to INR 25,000 if the expense is already within the approved team budget. Department heads may approve purchases up to INR 100,000. Any purchase above INR 100,000 requires finance approval and director sign-off before the order is placed.

Preferred Vendors
Employees should use preferred vendors when available for laptops, cloud credits, office equipment, and collaboration software. If a preferred vendor cannot meet the required timeline or specification, the buyer must document the reason for using an alternative vendor.

Subscription Management
Recurring software subscriptions must have a named business owner. The owner is responsible for reviewing active seats every quarter and removing unused licenses. Multi-year contracts should not be signed without finance review.

Invoice Submission
Vendor invoices should be submitted to finance-ops@northstaranalytics.example with the purchase order number, billing contact, and payment due date. Missing purchase order numbers may delay payment processing.

Reimbursement Rules
Employee reimbursement claims must be submitted within 30 days of purchase. Claims older than 60 days require manager explanation and finance approval unless the delay was caused by a documented system issue.
